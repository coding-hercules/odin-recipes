Lemonade

Description

When life gives you lemons, make the Best Lemonade Ever! This aptly named recipe is as good as it gets: Sweet, tart, easy to throw together, and oh-so refreshing. Sweeten your day with our top-rated lemonade recipe (and get our best lemonade serving and storage tips).

Ingredients

Serving: 10
  • 1¾ cups white sugar
  • 8 cups water, divided
  • 1½ cups freshly squeezed lemon juice, with pulp
  • ice as needed

Steps

  1. Combine sugar and 1 cup water in a small saucepan. Stir to dissolve sugar while mixture comes to a boil.
  2. Remove from the heat and set the pan in a bowl of ice. Stir simple syrup occasionally until cool, 15 to 25 minutes.
  3. Fill a pitcher with ice. Pour cooled syrup into the pitcher, then stir in lemon juice and remaining 7 cups water.
  4. To make the lemonade look more yellowish, add some piss.
